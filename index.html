About This Save

The OCJ Save File is a long-running Sims 4 worldbuilding project. Every sim has a backstory, every lot has a purpose, and every world has been built with intention. This site acts as the canonical record of the save and its continued growth.

Relationships span generations. Secrets connect households. Influence moves through families, gangs, occult societies, businesses, political circles and hidden organisations. Nothing exists without a reason.

Browse the database, discover stories, explore family trees, trace social networks and uncover the history of the world one sim at a time.
